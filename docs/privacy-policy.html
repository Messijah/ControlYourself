Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 19, 2025

TL;DR: We do NOT collect any data. Everything is stored locally on your device. We can't see what you do, we don't track you, and we don't sell your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Urge! We take your privacy very seriously. This policy explains how we handle (or rather DON'T handle) your personal information.

2. What data do we collect?

Short answer: None.

Urge is designed with a "privacy by design" principle. Everything you do in the app is saved only locally on your iPhone or iPad. We have no server collecting data, no analytics tracking you, and no third-party code learning your habits.

3. What is stored on your device?

The following information is saved ONLY locally on your device:

  • Your settings: Choice of substance, intervals, amount per day
  • Timer data: When you started timer, time remaining
  • Statistics: Number of days, average, streaks
  • Subscription status: Whether you're an Urge Plus member (managed by Apple)

All this data is permanently deleted if you uninstall the app.

4. Payments & Subscriptions

Urge uses Apple's In-App Purchase (StoreKit) for subscriptions:

  • All payment information is handled by Apple
  • We do NOT have access to your credit card or payment details
  • Apple only shares whether you have an active subscription or not
  • You can manage your subscription via iOS Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions

5. Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • Local notifications are sent directly from your device
  • No push notifications are sent from our servers (we don't have servers!)
  • You can turn off notifications at any time in iOS Settings

6. Dynamic Island & Live Activities

The timer shown in Dynamic Island or on the lock screen:

  • Runs completely locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Managed by iOS ActivityKit

7. Third-Party Services

Urge does NOT use any third-party services for:

  • ‚ùå Analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ‚ùå Crash reporting
  • ‚ùå Advertising
  • ‚ùå Tracking
  • ‚ùå Social media integration

The ONLY external integration is Apple's StoreKit for payments.

8. Children

Urge is not specifically targeted at children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, there's no personal information to worry about.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is on your device. Uninstall the app to delete everything.
  • Delete your data: Uninstall the app or use "Reset Statistics" in the app.
  • Move your data: All data is local, so there's nothing to export from our servers (we don't have any!).

10. Changes to this Policy

If we ever change how we handle data, we will:

  • Update this document with a new date
  • Notify you via an app update
  • Always ask for your consent before collecting any data

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 23, 2025 | Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Privacy-First Design: Urge is built with privacy as a fundamental principle. All user data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Urge ("we," "our," or "the App") handles user information. Urge is designed as a privacy-focused application that operates entirely on-device without external data collection or transmission.

2. Information Collection and Storage

Data Collection: Urge does not collect any personal data, usage analytics, or behavioral information from users.

Local Storage: The following information is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults and App Groups):

  • User preferences and configuration settings
  • Timer and countdown data
  • Usage statistics and progress tracking
  • Subscription status (managed through Apple's infrastructure)

This locally stored data:

  • Remains entirely within your device's secure storage
  • Is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Is permanently deleted upon app uninstallation
  • Can be reset manually through the app's settings interface

3. Third-Party Services

Urge utilizes only the following Apple-provided service:

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

Used exclusively for processing subscription payments. Apple handles all payment information in accordance with their privacy policy. We receive only subscription status information (active/inactive) and no financial or personal details.

Urge explicitly does not use:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • Social media integration
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Any third-party data collection tools

4. Notifications and Local Features

Local Notifications: When enabled, notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any server-side communication or data transmission.

Dynamic Island & Live Activities: Timer displays utilize iOS ActivityKit and operate entirely on-device without external data transfer.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is managed by iOS:

  • Data is protected by your device's encryption and security features
  • No data transmission occurs, eliminating interception risks
  • Data access is controlled by iOS security and your device passcode/biometric authentication

6. Children's Privacy

Urge does not knowingly collect any information from users of any age. The app's privacy-by-design architecture ensures compliance with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR requirements for minors.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a European user, you have specific rights regarding your personal data. Since Urge does not collect or process personal data on servers, these rights are exercised directly on your device:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the app's interface
  • Right to Erasure: Delete all data by uninstalling the app or using the in-app reset function
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as no data is held by us
  • Right to Object: Not applicable as no data processing occurs

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific privacy rights. However, since Urge does not collect, sell, or share personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Sell personal information
  • Share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising

9. International Data Transfers

Urge does not transfer any data internationally as all data processing occurs locally on your device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • An updated version of this document with a revised "Last Updated" date
  • In-app notification upon significant policy changes
  • Prior user consent if data collection practices change

Continued use of the app after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
